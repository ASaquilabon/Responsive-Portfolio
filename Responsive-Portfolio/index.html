About Me

Archie Saquilabon started freestyle dancing in the early 2000's. Inspired by the movie Rize, he chose to specialize specifically in the high energy style of krump. In 2008, he decided to take his talents toward choreography. Later in 2009, he then began his journey with dream team GRV. Given the opportunity, he infused his love for krump and choreography and debuted his first krump piece with GRV on the prestigious stage of The World of Dance: Los Angeles.

Today, he still lives his dream of being on GRV while looking to help spread his knowledge and infused style of choreography and krump.
